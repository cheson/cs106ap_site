Tiny feedback
2018011014

Hello! Tiny feedback (as inspired by a previous 106B professor Chris Gregg) is available starting today! As instructors, we constantly strive to improve our lectures and the class as a whole. We provide anonymous "Tiny Feedback" opportunities to the class after each lecture, and the feedback helps us guage how CS 106AP is going on a bit more personal level. We very much appreciate constructive feedback, and we want to know what is working and what you would like to see us do differently. We will read every piece of feedback and make every attempt to improve the course as the quarter progresses.
